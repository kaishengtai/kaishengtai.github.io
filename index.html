About

I'm currently a Senior Data Scientist at MetaMind. Recently, I was an MS student in the Stanford NLP Group, working on applications of deep learning to NLP under the supervision of Chris Manning. Previously, I received an AB in Physics from Princeton University.

Interests

I'm primarily interested in the following problems under the broad umbrella of deep learning for NLP: learning distributed representations of phrases and sentences, unsupervised representation learning in NLP, applications of recurrent neural network architectures, and question answering.
