About

I am a second-year MS student in Computer Science at Stanford. Previously, I was an undergraduate in Physics at Princeton.

Interests

I am interested in applications of neural network models to natural language processing tasks: to what extent can these techniques be leveraged in settings where it is crucial to have a precise understanding of the semantics of natural language utterances?

Specific topics include: learning distributed representations of text, unsupervised feature learning in NLP, applications of recurrent neural network architectures, and question answering.

Occasionally, I dabble in data visualization using d3.js.
