About

I'm a fourth-year Ph.D. student in the Future Data Systems group at Stanford University, co-advised by Peter Bailis and Gregory Valiant. My research focuses on machine learning, with an emphasis on representation learning and methods for incorporating prior knowledge into neural network architectures. I'm also interested in space-efficient sketching algorithms for machine learning applications.

Prior to starting the Ph.D., I spent a year working full-time at MetaMind, which was acquired by Salesforce in 2016. I received an MS in CS from Stanford in 2015. During my Master's, I was a member of the Stanford NLP Group. As an undergraduate, I studied Physics at Princeton University.
